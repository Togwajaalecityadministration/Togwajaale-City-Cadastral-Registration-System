## 📌 Overview

The Tog-Wajaale-City Administration Integrated Management System is a comprehensive, secure, and professional web-based platform designed to modernize and streamline city administration services. This system replaces traditional paper-based methods with a digital, efficient, and transparent solution.

---

## 🎯 Mission & Vision

### 👁️ Our Vision
*To be a model city administration in Ethiopia, delivering transparent, efficient, and citizen-centered services that promote sustainable urban development and economic growth.*

### 🚩 Our Mission
*To provide secure, accessible, and reliable administrative services through modern technology, ensuring the protection of property rights, revenue generation, and citizen satisfaction.*

## 📋 Modules & Features

### 🏛️ integrated Registry Module

| Form | Description |
|----------|-----------------|
| Land Transaction | Register land parcels with owner details, coordinates, area calculation, and transaction history |
| Deed Document | Manage official deed documents with owner, parcel, and financial details |
| Restricted Deed | Handle restricted deeds with bank lending information and 1% loan fee calculation |

Key Features:
- ✅ UTM Zone 38N Coordinates (4-point polygon)
- ✅ Area Calculation (Rectangular) 
- ✅ Auto File & Parcel Number Generation
- ✅ Owner & National ID Photo Upload

---

### 🛣️ Road Inventory Module

| Form | Description |
|----------|-----------------|
| Road Location | Record road segments with surface type, length, coordinates, and condition |
| Bridge Physical | Manage bridge inventory with structural details and condition |
| Roundabout | Track roundabouts with island area, entry points, and maintenance cost |
| Light Poles | Inventory street light poles with type, condition, and cost |
| Road Furniture | Record benches, signs, bus shelters, and other furniture |

Key Features:
- ✅ UTM Zone 38N Coordinates
- ✅ Condition Tracking (Good/Fair/Poor)
- ✅ Cost & Asset Value Management

---

### 🏗️ Building Permit Module

| Form | Description |
|----------|-----------------|
| Owner Form | Register building owner information and identification |
| Building Form | Record building details, dimensions, materials, and type |
| Tax/Payment | Track payments, receipts, and tax clearance |
| Reports | Generate daily, monthly, and quarterly reports |

Key Features:
- ✅ Building Area Calculation (L × W)
- ✅ Payment Method Tracking
- ✅ Tax Clearance Management
