 OVERVIEW

The Tog-Wajaale City Administration Cadastral Registration System is a comprehensive, secure, and professional web-based platform designed to modernize and digitize the entire land registration process. This system replaces traditional paper-based methods with a digital, efficient, and transparent solution, serving as the backbone of land administration in Tog-Wajaale City.

> "From Application to Certificate - One Complete System"

---

 MISSION & VISION

 Our Vision
*To be a model city administration in Ethiopia, delivering transparent, efficient, and citizen-centered cadastral registration services that promote sustainable urban development, secure property rights, and economic growth.*

 Our Mission
*To provide secure, accessible, and reliable cadastral registration services through modern technology, ensuring the protection of land rights, revenue generation, and citizen satisfaction.*
